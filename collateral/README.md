# DenverForAll.org — Funding Collateral

Application materials, outreach letters, and fundraising copy for securing funding for DenverForAll's policy research, civic tools, and organizing infrastructure.

## Priority Timeline (4–8 Weeks)

### Week 1–2: Fiscal Sponsorship (Unlocks Everything Else)
- `fiscal-sponsorship/social-good-fund-application.md` — Primary (faster, 8% fee, ~1 month to start)
- `fiscal-sponsorship/movement-strategy-center-inquiry.md` — Secondary (cohort-based, more support)

### Week 2–3: Grant Applications
- `grants/denver-foundation-community-grants.md` — $20K–$50K (deadline March 2, 2026)
- `grants/caring-for-denver-foundation.md` — $200K–$800K (opens March 2026)

### Week 2–4: Grassroots Fundraising
- `fundraising/donation-page-copy.md` — ActBlue/Givebutter page copy and donation tiers

### Week 3–6: Network & Partner Outreach
- `outreach/local-progress-colorado.md` — Join network of progressive local officials
- `outreach/movement-voter-project.md` — Partner application for organizing funding
- `outreach/fund-for-housing-opportunity.md` — $50K–$140K housing research grants
- `outreach/colorado-health-foundation.md` — ~$100M/year, medical debt + housing-as-health
- `outreach/undue-medical-debt-partnership.md` — Denver medical debt campaign partner
- `outreach/institute-local-self-reliance.md` — Municipal broadband research partner

### Reusable Across All Applications
- `organizational-profile.md` — Core org description, mission, metrics, evidence

## Note on Colorado Gives Foundation
The 2026 Housing Grant (deadline Feb 27) is **Jefferson County-specific**. DenverForAll primarily serves Denver County. This grant is not a strong fit unless we can demonstrate Jeffco impact. Deprioritized in favor of the Denver Foundation grant.
